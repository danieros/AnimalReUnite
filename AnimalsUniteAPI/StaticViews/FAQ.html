FAQ

Why use PetSociety and not some other social networking program like FaceBook?

First off we don't compete we compliment. We aim to provide you with a one stop assist for your pets. We Love animals and this project started off as a way to make a difference to the world of thousands if not hundred thosands pets that are lost, neglected, tortured, abused etc in South Africa. We know we can't report every lost dog or cat or help find every lost pet or report every abused pet but we can can use technology to help us. That is what we are good at and that is the medium we decided on for this project. In short, see us as a tool to help you help us looking out for them. (Confusing? Not really, if you replace them with our pets.)

Will this service cost me money?

Please read the section on pricing. Basically we asking for a donation of which part will be used for running the service and and part be donated to your favourite animal shelter.
