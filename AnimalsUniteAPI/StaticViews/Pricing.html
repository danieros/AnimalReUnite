Pricing

Pricing structure is very simple as essentially all services are free. We are animal lovers we really just trying to recover costs. This is more a passion than a business for us but the reality is that it does cost money to build, improve, host etc. So how does it work:
  • You can use any of the services and all we ask for is a once off R50 donation. (Completely voluntary)
  • R10 of your donation will be paid to your selected animal shelter and you will receive proof of payment.
  • You can register as many pets as you want linked to one email address and one location.
  • We will soon release be releasing a mobile app which will be free for anyone that made a donation.
  • We have a wall of heroes to thank everyone that has donated. Go and ahev a look.
