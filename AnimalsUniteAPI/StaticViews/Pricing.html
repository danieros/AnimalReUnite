Pricing

Pricing structure is very simple and as animal lovers we really just trying to recover costs. This is more a passion than a business for us but the reality is that it does cost money to build, improve, host etc. So how does it work:
  • Once of registration fee of R60
  • R10 of your registration fee will be paid to your selected animal shelter and you will receive proof of payment
  • You do not pay anything when registering but if haven't received your EFT transfer in 30 days from registration your account will be disabled
  • You can register as many pets as you want linked to one email address and one location
  • We will soon release mobile app which will be free for use
